My Journey

I'm a Computer Science graduate whose web development journey began in my third year of university, where I was introduced to the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. That same year, I interned at Instiq Professional Services, an experience that deepened my interest in front-end development and broadened my understanding of software development as a whole. Since then, I’ve continued to grow by building a range of small projects—experimenting with libraries, frameworks, and languages like Python (Flask) and Node.js. Now, as a student on the Front-End track at AltSchool Africa , I’m sharpening my skills and pushing toward professional certification, one project at a time.

My Goals...

My core goal is to establish a strong and lasting presence in the software development space, backed by continuous learning and professional certifications. While I’m currently focused on front-end development, I have a long-standing ambition to grow into a well-rounded full-stack developer. I also aim to explore mobile and standalone application development in depth, expanding my versatility by working with a wide range of frameworks and technologies. For me, it's all about building expertise that’s both broad and deep — and using that to create impactful, user-centered solutions.
